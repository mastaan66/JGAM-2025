About the Event

JAGAM 2025 organized in conjunction with the Diamond Jubilee Celebrations of JNTUH UCESTH is set to be a landmark event that brings together the global alumni community of JNTUH. This grand reunion offers a platform to reconnect with batchmates, revisit memories, and engage with the university's progress and vision.


With interactive sessions, cultural programs, and networking opportunities, JAGAM 2025 is more than a celebration — it’s a tribute to the shared journey of our vibrant alumni community.

Why Contribute?

Extending your contribution towards the JAGAM 2025, with a focus on the innovation/student-centric needs, provides opportunities to:

  • Fuel student innovation and Start-ups
  • Fund social innovation and impact
  • Build a legacy of innovation
  • Bridge the resource gap
  • Support equal opportunities (By funding for merit-based scholarships and student projects)
  • Boost career readiness of students
  • Be a part of JNTUH's growth story – To ensure it continues to produce leaders and innovators
  • Avail 80G & 12A Tax Exemption & CSR Certification
